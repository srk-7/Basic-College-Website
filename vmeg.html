About Institution

It is a UGC Autonomous college, approved by AICTE, and is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad. We offer undergraduate B.Tech programmes in CSE, CSE (AI&ML), AI&ML, AI&DS, IT, ECE, EEE, ME and CE; postgraduate M.Tech programmes with specialisation in DECS, PEED, CSE, SE, and ED; MBA programme; and doctoral programmes in CSE, ECE and ME.
